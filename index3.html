project crux

case study topic

orginazation and resource managment is arguably the most important part of any project especially in the tech industry which is riddled with complex folder structures and time restrictions.

the challenge

unifying orginazation methods

as you can see staying organized takes quite a few different components all of which exist in different places making the experience of staying organized a bit fractionated. rebooting a pc means having to relayout windows and diging for files in some ungodly file structure. for most the act of staying on track with projects is a meticulous time consuming task. my aim with this software is to make use of our highly developed spatial awarness to stream line tasks like sorting, schedualing and goal setting for projects.

  • Category: UX design for project managment app

review of existing software

Clear app

this app makes traditional todo lists obsolete with its nifty alarm feature. its also very easy to use and look at. however its very limited and not designed to help with scope and resource management.

axosoft

axosofts software is an awsome becuase it facilitates scrumb. however it doesnt provide the ability organize and store files and unlike the "clear" app its a bit intimidating and hard to navigate.

visual design process

the design

why it looks the way it does.

the design is focused on browsing labeling and placing large amounts of content. so i created a large infinate empty space. i used a skew morphic design which is reminscent of apples designs which are very popular in the tech world to create a sense of familiarity. i chose purple as the main interface color to invoke creative thought. this design uses the concepts of scrum much like the axosoft design. however the ui has been simplified by replacing words by recognizable icons which removes the need to read list after list of buttons.

the header is purple to invoke creativity. the ui has been as stripped down as possible to avoid visual cluter. project title front and center sorting, view port, and calander funtions to the left folder navigation, hide lock and delete functions to the right.bottom center favorates tabs bottom left individual container creation and labeling and editing controls as well as currently seleted indicator bottom right is the favorates bar controls search bar bottom right of the screen is the zoom control and the questionmark to the left is the legend toggle in case youve no idea what the icons stand for.
