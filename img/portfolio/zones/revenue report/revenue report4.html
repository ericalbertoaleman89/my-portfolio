Summary

The May Zones CustomerConnect business conference took place in Orlando, Fla. and drew record-breaking customer attendance. This was the first CustomerConnect event that reflected our transition from four CustomerConnect events a year to two. Opening it up to two events allows us to bring more customers together than ever before, helping us build deep relationships with the participants.

The theme for the May event was "Transform Your Infrastructure" and gave attendees a chance to meet Zones leaders and top partners, and hear testimonials from multiple Zones customers. You can view the press release describing the event here.

Special thanks go out to our entire team
